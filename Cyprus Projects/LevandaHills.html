The prestigious Levanda Hills project will be home to sixty-one luxurious villas with private pools and roof gardens. These properties have been designed with the discerning buyer in mind and offer either 3 or 4 bedroom accommodation that provides the ideal living environment for both permanent residency and holiday investment. Through innovation in design and the strategic positioning of each individual property, these luxury properties will make maximum use of natural light and shade to provide a fresh feeling and spacious living space. We have created a cool and relaxing environment that will make the maximum use of outdoor living during the long lazy Cyprus summers and provide a cosy home during the cooler months of the year. Our team of designers and architects have worked together to create a blend of traditional style and modern design features that owners on this exclusive development will be benefiting from for many years to come. This prime location has been chosen to ensure that these properties add value to both the environment they are situated in and the lifestyle of the astute buyers who choose to make them their homes.

The properties feature spacious garden areas that will make the most of Cyprusâ€™s South East Coast climate. Each property is offered with a diverse choice of interior finishes and layouts to complement every individual taste and they offer a perfect balance of form and function. Location and convenience have been priorities in our remit for the Levanda Hills Development. This is reflected in the number of shops, bars, supermarkets and waterfront restaurants that are within walking distance of the project. Set on a gently sloping hillock near the picturesque area of Ayia Triada, the location affords panoramic sea views and easy access to all the local amenities of the resort town of Protaras. The vibrant nightlife of Ayia Napa is only 15 minutes away by car and the bustling inland town of Paralimni with its trendy boutiques and friendly tavernas can be reached in around 10 minutes.

A regular bus service connects with the local resort of Kapparis and also services Ayia Napa, Paralimni, and Protaras. The local fishing harbour and quiet beaches of Ayia Triada is only a short walk away and the airport can be accessed in as little as 35 minutes of driving time thanks to the Islands modern motorway network. As with all of our properties, the Levanda Hills Project features properties that are built to the highest of standards using the most innovative construction methods and durable materials. By keeping the local landscape firmly in mind, we have achieved an enviable balance between the aesthetic value of the villas and the timeless rugged beauty of this typical Cyprus landscape. These stunning properties with covered areas from 133m2 to 195 m2 on plot sizes between 286m2 to 421m2 can be delivered key ready within 24 months of signing. Work on this exclusive development is already underway so prospective owners of property on the Levanda Hills project are urged to make a reservation at their earliest convenience.
